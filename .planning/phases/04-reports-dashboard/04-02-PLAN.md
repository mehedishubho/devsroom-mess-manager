## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-02-01 | Information disclosure (IDOR) | /my/reports/* routes | mitigate | Routes have NO `{member}` URL parameter. Controller derives `$member = $request->user()->getMemberOrNull()` — the member identity is structurally bound to the session, not the URL. Test: MyStatementTest::test_member_cannot_view_other_member_statement asserts acting as member A with `?member_id=B` in URL still renders only A's data. |
| T-04-02-02 | Information disclosure (peer dues/advances) | Member Monthly Report view (D-19) | mitigate | View omits the per-member `<table>`. Only aggregate sums (total_due, total_advance across all members) are exposed — individual members' dues/advances are NOT visible. Test: MyMonthlyReportTest::test_member_monthly_has_no_per_member_table asserts another member's name + their specific due amount are absent from response. |
| T-04-02-03 | Elevation of privilege | /my routes accessed by admin | mitigate | Routes wrapped in `['auth', 'role:user']` middleware. Admin → 403. Test: MyDashboardTest::test_manager_forbidden_on_my_dashboard + MyStatementTest::test_manager_forbidden_on_member_routes. |
| T-04-02-04 | Information disclosure (cache bleed) | bill-preview cache key | accept | Cache key is `bill-preview:{mess_id}:{YYYY}-{MM}` — scoped by mess_id (D-17). Member A and member B in the same mess share the same cache (correct — they share aggregates).
